Reengineering and Retrofitting of Products

NEON can also help you in procuring machinery/parts, which are out of the manufacturing range of original manufacturer, from India based on available drawings or samples. We would like to inform you that we are in a position to restore/rebuild your existing equipment without compromising its performance. In this process, our technical team visits the factory site and there experts study the equipment and its working condition, identifying the areas of trouble.

The process is followed while undertaking the retro fittings of the parts:

  • Our team from India will visit your factory/site.
  • Design and drawings will be taken on site.
  • Experts study of the working condition.
  • Obtaining MOC and the chemical composition required for manufacturing of the part.

We mention below few examples of our services rendered and machinery supplied to Sugar factories:

  • We have successfully manufactured, supplied and commissioned 2 Nos. of complete Mill Turbine Rotor Shafts with Blades of 1200 KW Turbines based on old Turbine Rotor and on-site actual dimensions taken by our Technical team.
  • We have also supplied Centrifuge Baskets, screens and other spares for SC 1350 KT - 30° Centrifugal Machines after preparation of as built drawings and understanding exact requirements of Sugar factory.
  • We have prepared manufacturing drawings of Pump spares like body, impeller, shaft, mechanical Seal etc. of various brands of Pumps for one of the Sugar Mills and thereafter we have also supplied them required Pump Spares to suit their existing Pump sets.
  • We have successfully manufactured and supplied Oil Filters, Mechanical Seals, Mill Roller Grooving Tools, Rubber and plastic moulded parts, special type of couplings etc. exactly as per client's samples and matching not only the material of construction but also the quality and dimensions.

Thus, we can take up the challenge of manufacturing and supplying of any part of machinery and spares with the help of expertise of our technical team and/or your samples to substitute various European Make/Brands.
